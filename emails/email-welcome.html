Welcome, Alex! We're really excited to have you in the community.
Each month, we'll introduce you to another new or expecting parent nearby. Think of it as a friendship starter pack: you'll both have each others' names, contact, and a list of fun activities to do by yourselves or together with your families.
In order to find the best matches for you, we've put together a profile. If you haven't already, fill out as much — or as little — as you want; we'll find you a match with whatever information we have.
Go to your profile
Here's what to expect from us each month:
1st to 5th of the month:
Opt into matching by choosing a topic and how you want to meet — online or offline. You can always skip a month at no charge and get your subscription extended automatically.
7th of the month:
Receive your match ! Your introduction, accompanied by a whimsical piece of art from our community.
Before the 14th of the month:
Request a rematch if it's not working out
21st of the month:
A friendly follow-up to meet with your match (This is an optional setting!)
